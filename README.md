# Quick Commerce Business Analysis using SQL

## Project Overview
This project analyzes quick commerce order data to understand business performance, delivery efficiency, customer satisfaction, discount strategies, and product demand patterns.

## Tools Used
- SQL (MySQL)

## Dataset
The dataset contains approximately **1 million orders** across multiple cities and companies.

## Analysis Performed

### KPI Summary
- Total Orders
- Total Revenue
- Average Order Value
- Average Delivery Time
- Average Customer Rating

### Business & Sales Analysis
- Revenue by city
- Orders by city
- Company performance
- Average order value by city

### Delivery Performance Analysis
- Average delivery time
- Fastest and slowest delivery cities
- Delivery performance by company
- Orders delivered within 30 minutes

### Customer & Rating Analysis
- Customer rating trends
- Ratings by city and company
- Age group ordering behavior

### Discount & Pricing Analysis
- Percentage of orders with discounts
- Impact of discounts on order value
- Discount strategies used by companies

### Order Behavior Analysis
- Average items per order
- Product category demand
- Payment method usage

## Key Insights
- The platform processed **1M+ orders**, generating approximately **₹57 crore in revenue**.
- The **average order value is ₹572**.
- **97% of orders are delivered within 30 minutes**, showing strong delivery efficiency.
- **Gurgaon generates the highest revenue**, while **Hyderabad has the highest order volume**.
- **Dairy products are the most ordered and highest revenue-generating category**.
- **Cash on Delivery is the most frequently used payment method**.
